Finnidpro is the brand operated by Diptimate Consultant Private Limited. About Finnid:

Welcome to Finnidpro

Finnid” is name of ERP for management of business engaged in finance, loan, saving accounts, fixed deposit & recurring deposit etc. Apart from providing solution core finance business activities, it facilitate the its users to remain complied with all the possible legal compliances.
